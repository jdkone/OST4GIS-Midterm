Has Marijuana Legalisation Changed the Geography of Drug Crime in Denver?

2013

Although the State of Colorado voted to legalize recreational cannabis in 2012, commercial sales to the public did not commence until 2014.

2014

Due to the lag between the vote for legalizing cannabis and the final rollout of regulations and approvals, many retailers began setting up shop prior to 2014. As a result, there was an explosion of cannabis retailers operating once the law took effect. This map shows the locations of all active retailers in 2014, with 671 in total.

2015

After a full year of legalization, Denver's cannabis industry continued to grow at a high rate, with 832 active retailers.

2016

By 2016, the rate of growth began to slow, with 880 active retailers.

2017

In 2017, three years after legalization, there were 903 active retailers in Denver. As the growth rate continues to slow, it seems Denver's cannabis industry may be reaching peak saturation.
